About Indy Kart

At Indy Kart, we offer an exhilarating and adrenaline-pumping go-kart experience right in the heart of Pine City Mall. Whether you're a racing enthusiast or just looking for some high-speed fun, Indy Kart is the ultimate destination for go-karting excitement.

Indy Kart is not just a go-kart company; it's a place where the thrill of racing meets the convenience of a mall. Our journey started with a passion for motorsports, and we've brought that same passion to Pine City Mall. Our mission is to provide you with a memorable racing experience.

Indy Kart at Pine City Mall is not just about racing; it's also a community hub for motorsport enthusiasts. We frequently host racing events, competitions, and special activities for both seasoned racers and newcomers.
