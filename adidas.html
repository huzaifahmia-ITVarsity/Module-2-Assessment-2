About Adidas

Adidas is a multinational corporation, founded and headquartered in Herzogenaurach, Germany, which designs and manufactures footwear, apparel, and accessories. The sporting goods company also owns a share of the German football club Bayern Munich.

Adidas is a leading retailer at Pine City Mall, offering a wide range of products including clothing, accessories, and more. We provide the best quality products and outstanding customer service.
