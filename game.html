About Game

At Game, we're not just a store; we're your go-to destination for everything you need to make your life more fun, convenient, and affordable. Nestled in the heart of Durban, we've been serving the local community for years, and we take immense pride in being a part of your everyday life.

Game is more than just a store; we're your one-stop-shop for a wide range of products, from electronics and home appliances to toys, DIY essentials, outdoor gear, and much more. We understand that our customers lead diverse lives, so we stock a diverse range of products to cater to your needs.

Customer satisfaction is at the core of our values. We're dedicated to providing you with the best shopping experience!

Game is proud to be a part of the Durban community. We actively engage with local charities, schools, and organizations, supporting initiatives that make our community stronger and more vibrant.
