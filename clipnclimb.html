About Clip 'n Climb

Featuring more than 20 different levels of difficulty to allow for personal progression and creative expression, Clip ‘n Climb will have you smiling and having a great time, while giving a full-body workout as you navigate your way up the wall.

Ultimately, the aim is to get from the bottom of each wall to the top in the fastest and most awesome way possible!

First developed and later introduced in New Zealand between 2004 and 2006, it was inspired by a desire to expand on the core experience of BOUNCE – a brand new category of action to revolutionize indoor climbing. It incorporates more than 20 different designs and climbing challenges with varying routes for beginners through to experts. There are over 50 Clip ‘n Climb centres worldwide, delivering WOW experiences and healthy challenging fun.
