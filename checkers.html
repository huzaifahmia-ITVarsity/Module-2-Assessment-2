About Checkers

Welcome to Checkers - Your Neighborhood Grocer.

At Checkers, we're more than just a grocery store; we're your community's trusted source for fresh, affordable, and high-quality groceries. We've been proudly serving Durban and its neighborhoods for years, and we're dedicated to making your shopping experience easy, convenient, and enjoyable.

Checkers has a rich history in Durban, with deep roots in the community. We started as a small, family-owned store and have grown into a beloved neighborhood grocer. Our commitment to providing the finest products and excellent service has been our guiding principle since day one.

We invite you to experience Checkers for yourself. Our store is conveniently located in the heart of Durban, making it easy for you to find all the groceries you need. Whether you're shopping for your family, a special event, or just stocking up for the week, Checkers is here to serve you.

Thank you for choosing Checkers as your neighborhood grocer. We look forward to being your trusted partner for all your grocery needs.
